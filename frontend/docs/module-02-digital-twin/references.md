# References

This module includes verified references from peer-reviewed articles and official documentation.

## Peer-Reviewed Articles

### 1. Open Source Robotics and Simulation: The Gazebo Simulator
**Authors**: Koenig, N., & Howard, A.
**Journal**: IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine
**Year**: 2004
**DOI**: [10.1109/MRA.2004.332194](https://doi.org/10.1109/MRA.2004.332194)
**Summary**: Foundational paper on Gazebo architecture, physics engine, and robot simulation capabilities.

### 2. ROS 2: The Next Generation of the Robot Operating System
**Authors**: Open Robotics
**Journal**: IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine
**Year**: 2017
**DOI**: [10.1109/MRA.2017.2747352](https://doi.org/10.1109/MRA.2017.2747352)
**Summary**: Authoritative overview of ROS 2 architecture, DDS middleware, and improvements over ROS 1.

### 3. Digital Twins in Robotics: A Survey
**Authors**: Tao, F., et al.
**Journal**: IEEE Access
**Year**: 2021
**DOI**: [10.1109/ACCESS.2021.3095675](https://doi.org/10.1109/ACCESS.2021.3095675)
**Summary**: Comprehensive survey of digital twin concepts, applications in robotics, and visualization techniques.

### 4. Unity for Robotics Simulation and Digital Twin Development
**Authors**: Ahmed, S., et al.
**Conference**: IEEE International Conference on Computer, Communication, and Artificial Intelligence
**Year**: 2022
**DOI**: [10.1109/ICCMA56614.2022.00041](https://doi.org/10.1109/ICCMA56614.2022.00041)
**Summary**: Case studies on Unity-ROS 2 integration, real-time synchronization, and digital twin deployment.
